What is SmileyMeter?

SmileyMeter is a one-tap mood feedback app for Android tablets at the point of experience (shop exits, reception, office mood walls, and similar). Visitors tap a mood; staff review simple on-device summaries. The MVP stores feedback on the tablet by default—no visitor accounts.
